About Us

Founded in 2002, PureGold Oil is a leading brand committed to delivering high-quality, healthy cooking oils. Our oils are cold-pressed, non-GMO, and sourced from organic farms.

We are dedicated to sustainability, community support, and nutritional wellness. Whether you're cooking daily meals or gourmet dishes, PureGold is the heart of every healthy kitchen.

Our Products

Sunflower Oil

Rich in Vitamin E, our sunflower oil is perfect for frying and baking. It has a mild flavor and high smoke point.

Canola Oil

Low in saturated fats, Canola oil is known for its heart-healthy properties and versatility in cooking.

Mustard Oil

Bold and traditional, mustard oil adds a punch of flavor and is ideal for Indian and Asian cuisines.

Health Benefits

  • Heart-Healthy: Our oils are rich in Omega-3 and unsaturated fats, promoting better cardiovascular health.
  • Vitamin-Rich: Loaded with vitamins E and K, they support skin health, immunity, and cell repair.
  • Preservative-Free: No added chemicals or preservatives – just nature’s purity.
  • Cold-Pressed: Retains natural flavor and nutrients unlike refined oils.
